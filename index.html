Basil Okpara

Founder, Bazz Group Ltd

Basil Okpara, popularly known as Mr. Bazz is the Founder and CEO of Bazz Group Ltd. A group in Nigeria that consists of Bazz Global Concept Ltd, Bazz Haulages Ltd, Bazz Drinks & Beverages Ltd and Bazz Media.With over 10 years experience as an entrepreneur in Nigeria and having built brands like Bazz Tomatoes, Bazz Safety Matches, Grandeur Wines, Bazz is rightly positioned to offer mentorship, advise and guidance on doing business in Nigeria. Bazz draws from a well of knowledge and wisdom in business, and from lessons learned from the failure of his first business where he lost over $100,000 (One Hundred Thousand United States Dollars) in the business on importation in Nigeria.

He has since bounced back stronger than ever and has been speaking in different forums using his experiences to inspire other entrepreneurs and help them to see the pitfalls in doing business and navigate around them thereby become successful in business. Mr. Bazz speaks is also a public speaker and Business consultant, working with companies and individuals to minimize losses and maximize profits and also proffers solutions to challenging problems.
